Festivals and Celebrations

Dashain

Dashain, the biggest Hindu festival, is celebrated with joy and family reunions across Sudurpaschim, marked by tika, jamara, and blessings.

Holi Festival

Holi, the festival of colors, is joyfully celebrated across Sudurpaschim Province, uniting people of all ages and communities. Marking the arrival of spring and the victory of good over evil, Holi is filled with vibrant colors, laughter, and cultural significance.
